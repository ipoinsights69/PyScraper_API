Landmark Immigration IPO is a bookbuilding of ₹40.32 crores. The issue is entirely a fresh issue of 56.00 lakh shares.

Landmark Immigration IPO bidding started from January 16, 2025 and ended on January 20, 2025. The allotment for Landmark Immigration IPO was finalized on Tuesday, January 21, 2025. The shares got listed on BSE SME on January 23, 2025.

Landmark Immigration IPO price band is set at ₹72 per share. The minimum lot size for an application is 1600. The minimum amount of investment required by retail investors is ₹1,12,000. But it is suggested to the investor to bid at the cutoff price to avoid the oversubscription senerio, which is about to ₹1,15,200. The minimum lot size investment for HNI is 2 lots (3,200 shares) amounting to ₹2,30,400.

Shreni Shares Limited is the book-running lead manager of the Landmark Immigration IPO, while Kfin Technologies Limited is the registrar for the issue.The market maker for Landmark Immigration IPO is Shreni Shares Limited.

About Landmark Immigration Consultants Limited

Incorporated in 2010, Landmark Immigration Consultants Limited is engaged in the business of Global Consultancy Services.

The company offers Global Education Consultancy for students seeking to study abroad and Immigration Consultancy for visas, tourism, business, and permanent residency, primarily in Canada.

The company offers global education and immigration consultancy through 9 fully equipped branches, partnering with over 30 Canadian institutions, with dedicated consultants and training facilities. Company has entered into 3 Franchise Agreements at Jammu, Jind, and Karnal.

Services:

  • Global Education Consultancy Services: Education Consultancy, Education Loan Guidance, and Scholarships, Admission application Formalities, Training for Language Proficiency Tests, Study/Schooling Visa.
  • Immigration Consultancy Services: Tourist Visa/Business Visa, Permanent Residency Visa.

As of September 30, 2024, the company has around 128 employees on a payroll basis.

Competitive Strengths:

  • One-Stop Solution for Visa Consultancy Services
  • Certifications and appointments by reputed universities obtained by Promoter and Company
  • Dedicated Team of Counsellors
  • Experience of the Promoters and senior management team.
