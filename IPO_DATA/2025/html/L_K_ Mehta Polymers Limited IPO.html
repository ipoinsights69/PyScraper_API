About L.K. Mehta Polymers Limited

Incorporated in 1995, L.K. Mehta Polymers Limited is engaged in trading and manufacturing of Plastic products.

The company is also involved in trading and reprocessing basic raw materials like polypropylene and polyethylene granules for various customers.

The company sells products under the brand name "Super Pack," reflecting its commitment to superior quality and building lasting customer relationships.

The company has strict systems to ensure timely delivery and minimal errors, with procedures to check orders at each stage, focusing on consistent service and customer loyalty.

Product Portfolio:

  • Ropes: Monofilament Ropes, Danline Ropes, Tape Rope, these products are used in non-ISI applications like transportation, agriculture, and packaging, as well as in high-risk sectors like shipping, mining, and heavy machinery transport.
  • Twines: Baler Twine, Packaging wine(Sutli), these are usually used in Agriculture, Harvesting, Stationary, Handicraft Industry, Retail packaging of consumer goods, household use.
  • Granuels: Polyethylene, Polypropylene, For reprocessing and manufacturing of plastic products.

As on 25th January, 2025, the company has 21 full time employees.

Competitive Strength:

  • Experienced Promoters
  • Generational Management
  • Quality Service
  • High Level of Customer Satisfaction
  • Existing Client Relationship

L.K. Mehta Polymers IPO Review (Avoid)

[Dilip Davda]  LMPL is engaged in the trading and manufacturing of plastic products/primarily ropes and twines. It posted growth in its top lines for the reported periods, but its bottom line marked erratic movements. Based on recent financials, the issue appears exorbitantly priced. The company is operating in a highly competitive and fragmented segment. Tiny paid-up equity capital post-IPO indicates longer gestation period.
