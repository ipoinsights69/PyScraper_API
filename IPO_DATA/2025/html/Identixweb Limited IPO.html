Identixweb IPO is a bookbuilding of ₹16.63 crores. The issue is entirely a fresh issue of 30.80 lakh shares.

Identixweb IPO bidding started from March 26, 2025 and ended on March 28, 2025. The allotment for Identixweb IPO was finalized on Tuesday, April 1, 2025. The shares got listed on BSE SME on April 3, 2025.

Identixweb IPO price band is set at ₹54 per share. The minimum lot size for an application is 2000. The minimum amount of investment required by retail investors is ₹1,02,000. But it is suggested to the investor to bid at the cutoff price to avoid the oversubscription senerio, which is about to ₹1,08,000. The minimum lot size investment for HNI is 2 lots (4,000 shares) amounting to ₹2,16,000.

Beeline Capital Advisors Pvt Ltd is the book-running lead manager of the Identixweb IPO, while Skyline Financial Services Private Ltd is the registrar for the issue.The market maker for Identixweb IPO is Spread X Securities Private Limited.

About Identixweb Limited

Incorporated in 2017, Identixweb Limited is a technology company specializing in Shopify app development and custom web solutions.

The company offers services including Shopify app development, web app development using PHP and React, and WordPress plugin development.

With over 50 professionals, Identixweb has developed more than 35 public Shopify apps and completed over 100 projects, serving various industries such as e-commerce, fashion, fintech, and SaaS.

Services:

  • Shopify Application Development: The company builds Shopify apps to enhance store functionality, streamline operations, and boost sales.
  • Node.js Development: The company uses Node.js to create fast, efficient servers for responsive websites and applications.
  • PHP Development: The company develops PHP-based websites and apps for dynamic, interactive, and database-integrated web experiences.
  • Web Application Development: The company creates user-friendly web applications for shopping, email, and social networking across devices.

Products Portfolio:

  • Stellar Delivery Date & Pickup: This app is a solution for order delivery management.
  • iCart Cart Drawer Cart Upsell: Grow your Shopify store's average order size with iCart's complete cart upsell & cart cross-sell solution for growth-focused Shopify merchants.
  • TablePress - Data Tables: TablePress allows you to create tables that can be embedded into blogs, pages, or text widgets with a simple shortcode.
  • WordPress Plugin: With this you can accelerate online sales with an outstanding WooCommerce upsell plugin, driving more conversions and boosting your revenue effortlessly.

As of February 28, 2025, the company has a total of 64 Employees.

Competitive Strengths:

Steps to apply in Identixweb IPO through Zerodha

  1. Visit the Zerodha website and login to Console.
  2. Go to Portfolio and click the IPOs link.
  3. Go to the 'Identixweb IPO' row and click the 'Bid' button.
  4. Enter your UPI ID, Quantity, and Price.
  5. Submit IPO application form.
  6. Visit the UPI App (net banking or BHIM) to approve the mandate.
