Capital Infra Trust InvIT is a book build issue of ₹1,578.00 crores. The issue is a combination of fresh issue of 10.77 crore shares aggregating to ₹1,077.00 crores and offer for sale of 5.01 crore shares aggregating to ₹501.00 crores.

Capital Infra Trust InvIT bidding started from Jan 7, 2025 and ended on Jan 9, 2025. The allotment for Capital Infra Trust InvIT was finalized on Jan 14, 2025. The shares got listed on BSE, NSE on Jan 17, 2025.

Capital Infra Trust InvIT price band is set at ₹99.00 per share . The lot size for an application is 150. The minimum amount of investment required by an retail is ₹14,850 (150 shares). About Capital Infra Trust

Established in September 2023, Capital Infra Trust is an infrastructure investment trust sponsored by Gawar Construction Limited. The InvIT was established to engage in activities and make investments as an infrastructure investment trust, as allowed under the SEBI InvIT Regulations.

The sponsoring company specializes in constructing road and highway projects across 19 states in India for various government bodies, including the NHAI, MoRTH, MMRDA, and CPWD.

As of December 2024, the sponsor company's portfolio of 26 road projects on a hybrid annuity mode (“HAM”)with NHAI, of which 11 are completed projects, including the five acquired assets which Sadbhav Infrastructure Project Limited erstwhile owned, and 15 under-construction projects.

The Trust received a rating of ‘Provisional CRISIL AAA/Stable (Assigned)’ from CRISIL Ratings Limited for the Trust NCD(s) and proposed long-term bank loan facility on November 11, 2024.

Objects of the Issue (Capital Infra Trust InvIT Objectives)

The Offer Proceeds will be utilised towards the following objects

  1. Providing loans to the Project SPVs for repayment/pre-payment of external borrowings, in part or in full from the financial lenders (including any accrued interest and prepayment penalty); and
  2. Providing loans to the Project SPVs for repayment of unsecured loans availed by the Project SPVs from the Sponsor.
