About Property Share Investment Trust REIT

Incorporated in June 2024, Property Share Investment Trust is a SEBI-registered small and medium real estate investment trust. The Trust's second scheme is PropShare Titania.

The Trustee of Property Share Investment Trust is Axis Trustee Services Limited.

The Trustee provides the following services as

  • Debenture trustee;
  • Security trustee,
  • Facility agent;
  • Escrow agency,
  • Custody services;
  • Trust and retention account;
  • Securitization trustee;
  • Share monitoring trustee;
  • Lender repayment trustee;
  • Digital escrow agency; and
  • Trustee of REITs, InvITs, AIFs and family trust etc., in the domestic market.
