About Knowledge Realty Trust

Knowledge Realty Trust is the largest office REIT in India, based on Gross Asset Value (GAV) worth Rs 619,989 million as of March 31, 2025. It is also the second largest office REIT globally in terms of leasable Area.

Our Portfolio comprises 30 Grade A office assets totalling 46.3 msf as of March 31, 2025, 37.1 msf of Completed Area, 1.2 msf of Under Construction Area and 8.0 msf of Future Development Area.

Its portfolio has 6 city-centre offices and 24 business park/centres. Its portfolio of assets offers a wide range of amenities, such as& multi-cuisine food courts, exclusive members-only clubs, indoor and outdoor sports facilities, medical clinics, creche, and other health and recreation facilities.

The company's portfolio assets are spread across 6 cities, including Hyderabad, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Chennai, Gurugram and GIFT City, Ahmedabad. The company has a Committed Occupancy of 91.4% as of March 31, 2025.

It has a diversified tenant mix of prominent multinational tenants, including Fortune 500 companies, Global Capability Centers (GCCs), and leading domestic corporates.

Competitive Strengths

  • Largest office REIT in India by GAV and NOI and the most geographically diverse,
  • Strategically located in India’s top-performing markets
  • High quality assets with robust infrastructure and a wide range of amenities
  • Diversified tenant base with an increasing focus on leading domestic corporates and GCCs
  • Long-standing tenant relationships driving tenant retention and growth
  • Robust business model with strong embedded growth, stable cash flows
  • Renowned sponsors (Blackstone and Sattva) with global experience and local knowledge
